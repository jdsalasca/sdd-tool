# Requirement: Case Management Workflow

## ID
REQ-0701

## Objective
Track cases and documents with audit trails and role-based access.

## Actors
Partners, associates, paralegals

## Scope (in)
- Case intake
- Scheduling
- Document tracking

## Scope (out)
- Billing integration

## Acceptance criteria
- All document access logged
- Role-based access enforced

## Non-functional requirements
- Security: encryption at rest and in transit
- Performance: 500 concurrent users
- Availability: 99.9%

## Constraints
- Retention: 7 years

## Risks
- Privileged data exposure
