# Functional Spec: Case Management Workflow

## Overview
Manage case intake, scheduling, and document tracking.

## Actors
Partners, associates, paralegals

## Use cases
- Create case
- Upload documents
- Schedule hearing

## Flows
- Intake -> review -> schedule

## Business rules
- Access by role only

## Errors and exceptions
- Missing required documents

## Acceptance criteria
- Cases cannot be created without required documents
