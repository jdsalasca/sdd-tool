# Functional Spec: Coffee Shop Brand Kit

## Overview
Provide brand assets for print and social channels.

## Actors
Owner, designer

## Use cases
- Apply logo on menus
- Use social templates for posts

## Flows
- Draft -> review -> final delivery

## Business rules
- Fonts must be licensed
- Colors must pass contrast checks

## Errors and exceptions
- Missing assets block delivery

## Acceptance criteria
- Assets match requested formats
