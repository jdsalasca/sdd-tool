# Functional Spec: Coffee Shop Brand Kit

## Overview
Deliver a cohesive brand kit for digital and print channels.

## Actors
Owner, designer

## Use cases
- Logo usage on menus
- Social media campaign templates
- In-store signage

## Flows
- Concept approval
- Draft review
- Final delivery

## Business rules
- Colors must meet contrast guidelines.
- Fonts must be licensed.

## Errors and exceptions
- Missing assets block delivery.

## Acceptance criteria
- Deliverables match requested formats.
- Brand guidelines documented.
