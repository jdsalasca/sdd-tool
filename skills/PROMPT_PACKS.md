# Prompt Packs (question banks)

## Discovery questions (mandatory)
- What is the measurable objective?
- Who are the users and actors?
- What is in scope and out of scope?
- What acceptance criteria prove success?
- What non-functional requirements matter (security, performance, availability)?
- What data is sensitive or regulated?

## Ambiguity checks
- Replace vague adjectives with metrics.
- Ask for scale (users, traffic, data size).
- Identify external dependencies or integrations.
- Identify deadlines and compliance constraints.

## Risk prompts
- What can fail and what is the impact?
- What must never happen?
- What trade-offs are acceptable?

## Planning prompts
- What is the minimal viable architecture?
- What interfaces and data models are required?
- What are the top 3 risks and mitigations?

## Verification prompts
- What are the critical test scenarios?
- What is the minimum coverage target?
- What error cases must be tested?
